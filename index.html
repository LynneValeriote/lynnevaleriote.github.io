4. Ford Motor Company of Canada

The Ford Electric social media campaign was intended to create awareness of Ford’s electric vehicle offering and to "jolt" people in what they expect from Ford. Our team came up with a series of quick 10- and 15-second “whimsical" spots meant purely to grab attention and showcase Ford in the EV space. The core idea was that electricity isn’t a passing fad for powering vehicles. Here's the video script I wrote for the campaign. View Ford EV script (pdf).

I've also developed copy for many other Ford and Lincoln projects. Between 2012-2017, I did ongoing editing and Canadianization work for both the Ford and Lincoln model year change-over vehicles, including all of the cars, CUVs and SUVs, and trucks.

My challenge was to find Canadian-specific product and regulatory information, and encourage consumers to engage in a dialogue with Campbell's. View What's In My Food site (jpg). 6. BMO Financial Group

For two years, I researched and wrote curriculum for the BMO Institute for Learning - Global Board of Directors Learning App. I distilled complex financial industry-related subject matter into concise, easy-to-understand curriculum for board members to reference in preparation for global board meetings. Each quarterly release featured current affairs articles from five content pillars: financial industry, regulatory, business of banking, risk, and products & services. This BoD release sample focuses on the emergence and versatility of blockchain technology in the financial industry. View The Blockchain release deck (pdf)

7. World Vision

I helped write and launch the award-winning No Child for Sale website (pdf) and created content updates for it, post launch. The goal was to create a fresh, slightly edgy and in-your-face site that would made an impact, and raise awareness on the issues of child slavery and ethical supply chains. I also researched and wrote this Ethical Chocolate infographic (pdf) about child labour in the chocolate industry.

8. Playtex Sport Period FAQ

Playtex wanted a stronger FAQ section for their site to both engage and inform pre-teens and teens, as well as their parents/guardians. I brainstormed, researched, and wrote the copy for this section of the site alongside other marketing initiatives at the time. In writing this, I was mindful of tone-of-voice for the tweens and teens of today, and their parents. I did not assume that everyone had a "mom and a dad" and therefore I tried to show an awareness of the new reality of blended families. View Playtex Sport Period FAQ site.

9. TD Canada Trust - Get Saving

The TD Get Saving microsite (pdf) was designed to engage Millennials to start investing their money. TD wanted a fresh approach that would speak to this demographic. Leveraging the YouTube live-blogging videos emerging at the time, we pitched the premise of a financially savvy video blogger who's created her own video series to demystify and explain the bank's financial instruments, and share how she's been using them to help reach her own financial milestones.

I wrote the video scripts for "Get Saving", "Start Early", "Tools to help you Save", "What is an RSP?" and "RSPs: For much more than retirement". I also collaborated on some of the calculators, and I wrote many of the savings tips, and all of the RSP and TFSA FAQs. View the "Start Early" script (pdf).

10. TD VISA Direct

I brainstormed concepts with an Art Director, and wrote the video script and site copy for this primer on transferring money overseas using VISA Direct. Our goal was to appeal to the perspectives of New Canadians concerned with finding better ways to send their money to family members back home. View TD VISA Direct video script (pdf).

I've worked on many other TD Canada Trust projects like the TD Music Access site, TD Waterhouse Mobile Optimization, TD Cross Border Banking, TD RRSP Online Content, UGO Wallet App and more.
